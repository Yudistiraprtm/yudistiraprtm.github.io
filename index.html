HR Dashboard — MySQL & Power BI
End-to-end HR analytics project analyzing over 22,000 employee records spanning 2000–2020. Raw data was cleaned and analyzed in MySQL Workbench, then visualized in an interactive Power BI dashboard covering demographics, turnover, tenure, and workforce trends.
Key Findings
  • Male employees outnumber female; White is the most dominant race while Native Hawaiian & American Indian are least represented.
  • Majority of employees fall in the 35–44 age group; youngest is 23, oldest is 60.
  • Most employees work at HQ rather than remotely; average tenure for terminated employees is ~10 years.
  • The Auditing department has the highest turnover, followed by Legal and R&D. Marketing and HR have the lowest.
  • Gender distribution across departments is relatively balanced, though males are more prevalent overall.
  • Most employees originate from the state of Ohio; net employee count shows a sideways trend over the years.
  • Sales and R&D have the highest average tenure (~10 yrs); Legal, Auditing, and Training have the lowest.
